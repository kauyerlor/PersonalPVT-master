Personal PVT

Psychomotor Vigilance Task - PVT - testing can assess sleep deprivation levels. It does this by detecting periods of lapsed concentration, called "microsleep" (seriously), which are linked with tiredness.

Studies often have participants do the task over 10 times a day, but if you are less concerned with rigor, even just one or two tests a day can provide interesting results.

Note that this site uses your browser to store data, so you don't need to create an account. However, if you switch browsers, you won't be able to access your data unless you export it.
